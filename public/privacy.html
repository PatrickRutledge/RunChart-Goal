Privacy Policy for Run Chart Analytics

Last updated: July 11, 2025

Introduction

Run Chart Analytics ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our Android application.

Information Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data (including production numbers, goals, and chart settings) is processed locally on your device and is not sent to any external servers.

Data Storage

All chart data and settings are stored locally on your device. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the application.

Required Permissions

Our app requires minimal permissions:

  • Internet access: Only required for app updates through the Play Store
  • Share functionality: Only when you choose to share a chart

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
